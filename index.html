why?

The web changes constantly. Pages move, links disappear, and context shifts. Data from the web evidences cultural, political, and social experience. However, the purpose of most websites is to disseminate information, not to keep it.

POWER. Before it disappears, you can gather it!!

Next

who?

Cultural heritage institutions capture the web at a large scale. These projects harvest content following broad selection criteria, and then deliver it in a format that is optimized for browsing. Consequently, it does not serve all purposes.

Happily, you can use tools to build collections in pursuit of exploring your own questions!
